Aleria

A fantasy generator bound to a single world.

Knowledge is power, and power is survival.

Story scenario

Generates a scene featuring the canonical cast - Erdan, Mira, Night, Nare, Seraya, Rei, Ren-Ro, the Pope, the Grand Priests, and the Iron Queen - in places and conflicts the world bible already supports.

Character profile

Pulls one of the named cast and shows the canonical profile from the lore.

Skill

Skills cannot be taught. They are unlocked through specific, often obscure life conditions. Grades run Budding, Low, Neutral, Medium, High, Grand, Master, Null.

Spell

Full incantation: Trovak enshari korim thalos + suffix. Skilled casters compress this to the high-risk Tro-Ko form. Elemental spells imitate reality through mana - they are not the real thing.

Monster encounter

Star-rankings: 1 (one person) up to 7 (historical calamity). Only canonical monsters appear here.

Relic

Lost Magic items left by the Ancients in deep dungeons. Effects are random and powerful; their limits are part of the find.

Race

Canonical races and Devil subtypes from the world bible.

Location

Canonical places: continents, the Underworld, dungeons, and key civic sites.

Bound to the Aleria world bible. No characters, races, or systems outside that bible are used here.
